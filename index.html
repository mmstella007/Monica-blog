About Me

Hi, I'm Monica

By day, I'm a Digital Product Owner — someone who lives inside roadmaps, sprint retrospectives, and an embarrassing number of open browser tabs. But when the laptop finally closes, I become something else entirely: a curious, quietly restless person who chases good food, slow mornings, honest conversations, and the kind of travel that rearranges your perspective a little.

This space is not a portfolio. It's not a brand strategy. It's just… me. The version of me that still gets unreasonably excited about trying a new recipe, cries during films I've seen three times, overthinks everything, and genuinely believes a home-cooked meal can fix a hard week.

I started writing here because writing is how I make sense of the world — and I wanted somewhere honest to put it all. If something here resonates with you, even quietly, then this corner of the internet has done exactly what I hoped.
